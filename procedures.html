How the split works

Two passes, one rule

1
Separate reference from procedure. Definitions, safety, equipment, layout and roles are shared knowledge — not tasks.
2
Tag every procedure by job function. Some belong to two roles (Clamp Unload = Operator + Dock Associate), so the tag is a set.

One procedure, four layers

The SOP frames the process. The work instruction is the atomic step-by-step. Training and Observation both derive from it — you train and audit concrete steps, never the process in the abstract.
